omics/pbmc3k.h5ad
-----------------

Source: 10x Genomics, 3k PBMCs from a Healthy Donor.
URL: https://www.10xgenomics.com/datasets/3-k-pbm-cs-from-a-healthy-donor-1-standard-1-1-0
License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

The bundled AnnData file was generated from the 10x Genomics PBMC3k filtered
gene-barcode matrix using `bt.omics.io.load("pbmc3k")` and stored with
HDF5 gzip compression.
